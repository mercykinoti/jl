Areas Of Practice

We offer a full complement of contentious and non-contentious legal services in the following fields of law:

Banking & Finance

We advise domestic and international banks and lending institutions on specialized banking transactions, including syndicated loans, structured finance and secured debt.

Contract Law

The firm has extensive experience in commercial contract legal issues, contract drafting and breach of contract litigation. Based on our in-depth understanding of specific contractual requirements and the client’s needs, we draft contracts that address legal requirements and at the same time meet and protect the client’s interests. We draft and advise on a broad spectrum of commercial contracts, including aircraft purchase transactions, sale and lease contracts, partnership contracts, agency contracts, construction contracts, banking contracts and employment contracts as well as production, distribution, licensing, marketing, broadcast, advertising and intellectual property agreements.

Competition and Regulatory

We advise on all aspects of competition law with a focus on merger control, abuse of dominant market position, distribution systems and cartel investigations. We also advise across all regulated industry sectors on a broad range of matters, such as compensation claims, cost-oriented pricing, significant market power, state aid and unfair business practices.

Corporate & Commercial Law

The firm advises clients across different industries and sectors on corporate set-up and organization, corporate finance, corporate compliance and governance, mergers and acquisitions, business transactions including drafting and negotiating of all types of commercial contracts.

Corporate Governance and Compliance

We routinely advise our corporate clients on all government corporate and regulatory requirements to ensure they maintain good standing. Our experience covers, inter-alia, board of director responsibilities, board of directors and general assembly meetings, business licensing, compliance, corporate disclosure and governance. As in other areas, we act proactively and preventively but are highly competent and well experienced in representing executives and corporations in disputes related to the discharge of responsibilities and operations. The firm advises public shareholding companies (listed companies) and private shareholding companies on corporate governance and compliance matters.

Debt Management

Financial distress can have far reaching implications for corporate and individuals alike and, in the current economic climate, stakeholders are looking at ways to reduce costs through the use of various corporate or financial restructuring tools. We work with businesses in financial difficulties across a range of sectors with the view to rescuing such businesses, mitigating risk and maximising recoveries.

We act on both contentious and non-contentious matters for a variety of stakeholders which include debtors, funders and other creditors, insolvency practitioners, directors and parties interested in acquiring distressed assets or debt.

Dispute Resolution

Our litigation department represents clients before all trial and appellate courts in the Kingdom. Our team is able to litigate corporate and commercial disputes, regardless of size, complexity and subject matter and has expertise across practices and industries, including breach of contracts, labor and employment, construction, government procurement, intellectual property, real estate, distribution and agency, banking, white collar crime and corporate fraud.

The firm also has extensive experience in representing clients before local and international arbitration tribunals.

Employment & Immigration Law

We assist clients in respect to all employee legal matters, including advice on labor law, civil service and social security matters, drafting employment contracts and internal manuals and representation in disputes. We also help implement labor transfer and employee stock option plans and assist family businesses in enhancing governance by separating family ownership from management and control.

Based on the demand of our corporate clients, individual executives and professionals, we provide legal services in connection with visa petitions, investor’s immigration visa, national interest waiver petition, labor certification, and other related immigration matters.

Insurance Law

We are capable of handling insurance law related issues, including reviewing and drafting insurance contracts, advising on insurance regulatory issues and representing clients in various kinds of insurance litigation.

Intellectual Property Law

Our Intellectual Property (IP) team offers clients a comprehensive range of IP services, including IP rights advisory, registration, maintenance and administrations search and watch services, franchising, licensing and litigations. We routinely handle administrative oppositions and cancellations.

Private Client Services

Our firm understand the importance of tailoring to a client’s specific needs in order to achieve wealth preservation and distribution in the most efficient manner. We have established long term relationships with clients as they acquire and accumulate assets and experience life changes. We also assist clients in the administration and settlement of an estate so that distribution can be achieved in the most efficient manner as allowable by law. Our major areas of expertise are drafting wills and trust documents, evaluating the role of family limited partnerships and limited liability companies in transferring wealth and minimizing estate taxes.

Real Estate & Property Law

JB Chambers LLP has recently seen an increased need for sophisticated legal services to support ever more complicated development and finance models and transactions in the real estate sector and our firm has been actively involved in this development.

The firm represents companies in the construction, engineering and architecture fields and has an impressive track record in advising clients on all relevant issues.

Sector-related Services

Consumer Goods & Retail, Energy & Resources, Funds & Investment Management, Healthcare, Life Sciences & Chemicals, Industrials, Private Equity, Transport & Logistics, Hospitality.

Securities & Capital Markets Law

We advise clients on all aspects of transactions governed by Kenya's Securities Laws and Regulations, including securities listing and delisting, share issuance and underwriting, initial public offerings, public and private placements, securities trading and dealings, corporate governance and disclosure requirements.

Technology and Data Protection

We regularly advise clients in challenging, complex and high-profile assignments within the Technology sector. We also advise software vendors and other technology suppliers on a broad range of matters ranging from the development and launch of new products and services, to commercial agreements and intellectual property strategies

We provide data management, data and cyber security, and privacy law advice to a wide-ranging client base. Our services include advisory and project work in drafting and negotiating data processing and transfer agreements, responses to disclosure and access requests, and data protection audits and risk assessments. We also advise on the drafting and implementation of privacy policies, notices and consents, and compliance programmes and training.
