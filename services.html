Our Services


Company Secretarial Services


1 hr | Consultation Meeting

We offer the following services for your company: Statutory Company Secretary; attendance at Board Meetings and Committee Meetings; seal custody; custody of statutory registers of directors and shareholders; filing of annual returns; taking minutes at Annual General Meetings and Board of Directors Meetings; attending to changes in capital; drafting of Board Charter; constitution of committees and drafting terms of references; attending to changes in shareholding or directorship of a company.


Corporate Governance


1 hr | Consultation Meeting

Our firm offers the following services: secretarial audits; Corporate Governance audits; Board Evaluation; Corporate Governance Training; Compliance with corporate governance codes and principles; developing corporate governance reports; development of a comprehensive compliance program.


Corporate Services


1 hr | Consultation Meeting

We offer the following corporate services: incorporation of a legal entity in Kenya; registration of branch and representative office; provision of nominee director and branch’s agent; Provision of registered office; preparation and filing of annual returns in Kenya; Provision of initial secretarial matters for holding the first board meeting and shareholders’ meeting; provision of secretarial services to comply with routine filling requirements with Registrar of Companies; maintenance of statutory records; updates on changes to statutory requirements.


Handling Services


1 hr | Consultation Meeting

Legal Audits
